About Us

Whenever you're ready, we're here to help you book counselling online with
qualified, professional counselors and psychotherapists easily and quickly, at the
times and prices that suit you, wherever you are.

Our vision is a healthier, brighter world where everyone has access to
professional psychological support.

To make this possible, we're on a mission to enable people to quickly and easily
connect with the highest-quality wellbeing help, through our trusted community
of professional therapists and best-in-class mental health resources.

As the largest and most reputable psychotherapy group in the UK, we will
continue transforming the world one person at a time, for the good of everyone.

About Our Therapists

To help you find a therapist who is right for you, we ensure that all the therapists on our platform are
UK registered, insured and professionally trained counsellors, psychotherapists, psychologists and
cognitive behaviour therapists. They are skilled, experienced, compassionate, and dedicated to both
their personal and professional development. Our therapists offer appointments online by video and
chat, in person or over the telephone, because we know that talking to a real person, in real time, is
important to build a therapeutic relationship.

While the therapists on our platform might be diverse in their therapeutic approaches, they are
united in their exceptional commitment to clients.

Our Goals & Values

We believe that therapy can help anyone to transform their life. We want to make booking counselling with qualified therapists simple, fast and accessible to everyone, wherever they are.

We believe in the human potential for change and improvement and the importance of personal growth. We want to promote psychological well-being and demystify counselling and psychotherapy through shared knowledge, education and therapeutic connection.

We are on a mission to eliminate prolonged emotional suffering worldwide. We want to make it as common and important to seek treatment for our mental health as it is for physical difficulties. We are committed to maintaining the highest quality standards across all of the therapists listed on the platform and regularly review client feedback.

Our Client Guarantee

We aim to align ourselves with the most select group of therapists.
They are fully qualified, accredited and insured to practice privately. If
there's any reason why you're not getting the most out of your
sessions, let us know and we'll do our best to arrange an alternative
meeting at no cost to you.
